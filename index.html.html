About Us.

  • The staff and parents of the Golden Bell English High School are committed to establish a safe and positive environment of learning that lays emphasis on intellectual and emotional growth in which every student discovers and realizes his full potential and hence the highest academic standards are achieved. We lay strong emphasis on inculcating values – integrity, ethics, moral courage, hard work, responsibility and self-discipline. Golden Bell English High School has been established on this foundation to prepare the child for life. But, Rome was not built in a day and we are still growing… without any external aid or donations! We are just growing, with our commitment towards perfection; for we believe – that which is true, good and beautiful, shall always be accepted. Golden Bell English High School is set to provide creative and progressive education to rise above the conventional teaching in a peaceful and safe environment.
